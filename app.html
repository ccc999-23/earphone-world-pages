About this app

Internal publishing tool for Earphone World JP (@earphone_world_jp). Supports two modes: Inbox Draft (video.upload — saves to creator inbox for review before posting) and Direct Post (video.publish — publishes a photo slideshow directly to the connected account's TikTok profile).

Required products: Login Kit (user.info.basic), Content Posting API (video.upload, video.publish).
