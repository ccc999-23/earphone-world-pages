About this app

This is the internal publishing tool of Earphone World JP, a music curation account on TikTok (@earphone_world_jp). The tool sends slideshow images to the connected TikTok account's inbox as a draft for further editing inside the TikTok app.

Required products: Login Kit (user.info.basic), Content Posting API (video.upload).
