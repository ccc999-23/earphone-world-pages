Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Earphone World JP ("we," "our," or "the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our TikTok content posting tool. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you authorize the Service via TikTok's OAuth 2.0 flow, we receive the following information from TikTok:

  • TikTok Open ID — A unique, anonymized identifier assigned by TikTok to identify your account within this application
  • Display Name — Your TikTok display name, used to confirm account identity
  • Avatar URL — A link to your TikTok profile picture, displayed during the authorization process
  • Access Token and Refresh Token — OAuth tokens used to interact with TikTok's API on your behalf. These are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

We do not collect any other personal information, including email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account and confirm authorization
  • Upload photo slideshow content to your TikTok inbox as draft posts on your behalf
  • Display your account information during the authorization and posting process

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond the core functionality of the Service.

4. Data Storage and Retention

OAuth tokens (access token and refresh token) are stored locally on the user's device in a file with restricted permissions. No user data is stored on external servers or cloud databases operated by Earphone World JP.

Data obtained via TikTok APIs is not retained beyond the active session, except for OAuth tokens which are retained locally to avoid requiring re-authentication on each use. You can delete these tokens at any time by removing the local token file or revoking access via TikTok's account settings.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties, except as described below:

  • TikTok — We interact with TikTok's API to provide the Service. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal requirements — We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal process.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we handle, including storing OAuth tokens in local files with restricted file system permissions (mode 0600). However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You are responsible for maintaining the security of the device on which the Service is used.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — You may request information about the data we hold about you
  • Deletion — You may request deletion of your data by contacting us or by revoking the application's access via TikTok's account settings, which will invalidate all stored tokens
  • Revocation — You may revoke the Service's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's Connected Apps settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at: purepure.dog2223@gmail.com

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with TikTok's API. This Privacy Policy does not apply to TikTok's own data practices. We encourage you to review TikTok's Privacy Policy for information about how TikTok handles your data.

10. Changes to This Policy
